The TESTTOKEN Machine: Zero-Inflation Model
Unlike traditional inflationary "farm" tokens, TESTTOKEN rewards are not minted out of thin air. The ecosystem sustains itself through a Fixed-Supply Reward Pool:

Non-Inflationary: The total supply is capped. Rewards are distributed from a pre-funded Treasury.

The 45-Day Lock: Capital stay-time is rewarded. Early exits via the Emergency Exit function trigger a 1% penalty.

Penalty Redistribution: Fees from emergency exits are cycled back into the reward pool, further incentivizing long-term stakers without increasing total supply.

Treasury-Backed: 100% of rewards are accounted for at the time of staking initiation.
